Welcome to Break Bread Bakery! Come in and break some bread with us. Our staff will make you feel like family. We bring tradition, fun, and history into our business, taking pride in our warm and inviting setting for all. We strive to be constantly and consistently friendly, attentive, and courteous. We want each customer’s experience to be full of delight, and we focus on all aspects involved: great food, gracious service, warm atmosphere, heady aromas, and irresistible displays. We can’t wait to see you!

All our baked goods are made fresh daily! Our amazing bakery staff works tirelessly to deliver the best quality products to our customers. We only use the best and natural ingredients for our delicious treats. All of our sourcing is local, and we take pride in being part of the community. Come sit and enjoy a great treat.

Or make life sweeter... Eat dessert first! Sweets, cakes, and pies are made with love and dedication - and it shows! We are passionate about our lucious, sugary goodnesses. Give into your temptations and taste our amazing desserts, fit for any sweet tooth!
