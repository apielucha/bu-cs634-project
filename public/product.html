Our Products

The bakery was established by two siblings with the initial idea of selling fresh bread. Every Sunday during family dinner there would be a fresh loaf of bread that would be broken into pieces for everyone to share. The siblings used their family tradition of breaking bread during a family meal as their inspiration and vision for the bakery. The siblings wanted to create a bakery known to their customers as the place to get fresh bread to take home for their own family or to simply sit in the store, “break bread” and share in an experience of some delicious bread. As the business continued to grow, they expanded their market by selling other products, such as cakes, cookies, and pastries.
